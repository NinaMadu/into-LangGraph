**What Data Scientists Do:**

* **Collecting data:** Gathering data from various sources.
* **Cleaning and preparing data:** Transforming raw data into a usable format.
* **Analyzing data:** Using statistical methods and machine learning to identify patterns and insights.
* **Building models:** Creating predictive models to forecast future outcomes.
* **Visualizing data:** Communicating findings through charts and graphs.
* **Deploying models:** Implementing models into real-world applications.
* **Communicating results:** Presenting findings to stakeholders.
